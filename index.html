Smart scheduling · Natural light · Behavior nudges

When you reduce wasted electricity, you’re not only cutting your bill—you’re reducing the emissions required to power your home. Over the lifetime of a typical installation, that can add up to a significant reduction in your personal carbon footprint.

  • Smart schedules avoid heating water or cooling rooms you aren’t using.

  • Real‑time dashboards build awareness and better energy habits for the whole family.
